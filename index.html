The Silkfutures method

WE DON'T JUST SIGN VOICES. WE BUILD THEM.

Every artist who comes through Silkfutures leaves able to run a career, not just release a song. That means real craft, a clear voice, and the judgement to use a public platform responsibly — before the platform gets big.

01 — Discover

Open-door A&R

A monthly unsigned night and a demo inbox read by people, not a playlist algorithm.

02 — Develop

Craft & voice

Songwriting camps and vocal or production coaching to find an actual voice instead of a trend.

03 — Direct

Media training

Interview and public-speaking practice, so an artist understands the weight of an audience before they have one.

04 — Deliver

Studio & release

Studio time, mixing, mastering, visual identity and a release plan — via Silk Records or independently.

05 — Sustain

Aftercare

Royalty education and welfare support that carries on well after the first single drops.

How we keep it responsible

WE GROW PEOPLE, NOT JUST PROFILES.

A big audience is a responsibility before it's an asset. These five commitments run alongside every stage of Silkfutures, not just at the end of it.

Mental health & welfare

A named welfare contact for every artist, independent of their A&R relationship.

Financial literacy

Plain-English contracts and royalty splits, explained before anything is signed.

Media & public voice

Training on interviews and socials, so no one finds out how it works while it's live.

Creative ownership

Artists keep a real stake in their masters and publishing. We're a partner, not a landlord.

Long-term aftercare

We stay in the room for the second record, and the harder conversations too.
