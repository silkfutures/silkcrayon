No Harmful Music Policy

  • We do not record music that glorifies violence, exploitation, or the degradation of others — regardless of commercial precedent.
  • We believe authenticity is the most powerful artistic force. Music made from a real place always lands deeper.
  • We hold the same standard for every artist, from Silkfutures mentees to Silk Records acts.
  • Booking requests may be declined if the music doesn't align with these values. We'll always tell you honestly if that's the case.
